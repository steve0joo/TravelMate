Spotlight for 04/21 - Bali, Indonesia

By Phillip Abraham • April 21, 2025

Bali is more than beaches!

You may know Bali, Indonesia for its beautiful beaches, but there's so much more to see and do in this lively province! We've gathered a few places that are perfect for your next trip. Whether you're looking to immerse yourself in cultural activities, or simply relax on the beach, Bali has something for everyone. Take a look at our top picks for places to visit!

Places to visit in Bali

  1. Penglipuran Cultural Village: If you want to immerse yourself in a cultural experience, visit Penglipuran Cultural Village and take a tour of the many idylic temples!
  2. Denpasar: This beautiful city is the capital of Bali, and is the hub of the province! Go visit one of the bustling markets, or admire the natural beauty of this city!
  3. Sanur Beach: Wake up early enough, and you might get a chance to see the gorgeous sunrise over Sanur Beach!
